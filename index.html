The Story

Face Job is a collection of 10.000 NFTs representing the rebellious spirit of those who choose to celebrate life, rather than merely it.
They are profesional misfist, have a big heart, like to party, and are crazy enough to think they can change the Word. They have Flown under the radar, searching for like-minded creatives, artists, musicians, builders, and believes who dare to express their truth in a society that increasingly valvues rules anda confotmity.
